Derived from the Taino word “to listen”, Ocama beckons you to tune in to the symphony of nature. Perched on the hillside overlooking the Bay of Rincon, our 35-acre boutique retreat exudes tropical luxury living, privacy, and direct access to several of the most pristine beaches of the Samaná Peninsula. Ocama is the epitome of exotic elegance.

Villas

Our 8 private villas are exquisite three-story architectural masterpieces of understated contemporary luxury, blending seamlessly into the verdant tropical forest. Surrounded by winding trails and boasting breathtaking ocean views, they provide a rare oasis of tranquility and beauty.

Discover our villas

Dining

Experience the flavors of Dominican modern cuisine! Let us take you on a journey of the island’s heritage, its vibrant diversity and rich international influences. By delving into the roots of our culture, we offer a unique culinary journey that showcases local products and adapts to your personal preferences.
