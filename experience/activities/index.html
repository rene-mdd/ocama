    Activities

    Samana is known for its stunning seascapes and landscapes! On the coast, turquoise waters gently lap against palm-fringed sandy beaches. Inland, lush green mountains rise majestically, creating a dramatic backdrop against the clear blue sky. Waterfalls cascade down verdant slopes, and hidden caves invite exploration!

    It's a paradise for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility amidst breathtaking scenery!

    Hiking

    Ocama Retreat

    Walking in nature is good for mind, body, and soul!

    The Ocama Retreat is a large property, with most villas spaced well apart for privacy. As such, expect to do some good walking within the property.

    We encourage you to walk around using the stone paths designed to guide you to your villas, through the lush forest, and to the beach. Some exercise is involved, as the complex is located on a slope.

    7 Hidden Beaches of Samana

    Ocama is located on the trail of what is known as the “7 hidden beaches of Samana”, considered one of the best-kept secrets of the peninsula.

    This hiking adventure will take you through isolated virgin beaches, river systems, amazing scenery, and perfect spots for swimming and snorkeling, and even make it out as far as visiting the town of Las Galeras and Duarte cave!

    Whether you decide to take the hike through a self-guided tour, or by supporting the local guide association, have a fantastic time learning and creating memories in this beautiful part of the world, while contributing to the local economy, vital for the subsistence of our town.

    Boat Excursions

    If maritime and weather conditions permit, you can enjoy relaxation, revitalizing dips in the sea, or a number of adventures in and around our warm ocean waters. Discover remote beaches, nature reserves, hidden caves, and more on a beach-hopping boat tour exploring Playa Fronton, Playa Rincon, Playa Madama. Alternatively, embark on a boat trip to visit Los Haitises National Park.

    The boat excursions can be shared or private. A private excursion is always available, while for shared excursions we have to be able to form a group, which may not always be possible.
