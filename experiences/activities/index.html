ACTIVITIES

Samaná is known for its stunning seascapes and landscapes!
On the coast, turquoise waters gently lap against palm-fringed sandy beaches. Inland, lush green mountains rise majestically, creating a dramatic backdrop against the clear blue sky. Waterfalls cascade down verdant slopes and hidden caves invite exploration.
It’s a paradise for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility amidst breathtaking scenery!

Hiking within Ocama Retreat

Walking in nature is good for the mind, body, and soul! The Ocama Retreat is a large property, with most villas spaced well apart for privacy. Walking along stone paths through lush forests to travel between the villas and the beach offers a serene and scenic experience. The natural slopes add a fitness element, making each walk a bit of an exercise opportunity while enjoying the beautiful surroundings. Hiking within Ocama is a great way to stay active and do some “forest bathing” by mindfully engaging with the surrounding forest to relax and take in its sights, sounds, and smells.

7 Hidden Beaches of Samaná

Ocama is located on the trail of what is known as the “7 hidden beaches of Samaná”, considered one of the best-kept secrets of the peninsula. This hiking adventure will take you through isolated virgin beaches, river systems, amazing scenery, and perfect spots for swimming and snorkeling. This path can even take you towards the town of Las Galeras through the Duarte Cave. Whether you decide to take the hike through a self-guided tour, or by supporting the local guide association, have a fantastic time learning and creating memories, while contributing to the local economy, vital for the subsistence of our town.

Boat Excursions

If maritime and weather conditions permit, you can enjoy relaxation, revitalizing dips in the sea, or a number of adventures in and around our warm ocean waters. Discover remote beaches, nature reserves, hidden caves, and more on a beach-hopping boat tour exploring Playa Fronton, Playa Rincon, Playa Madama. Alternatively, embark on a boat trip to visit Los Haitises National Park. The boat excursions can be shared or private. A private excursion is always available, while for shared excursions we have to be able to form a group, which may not always be possible.

Whale Watching

Each year, from December to April, hundreds of humpback whales visit our waters, especially around Las Galeras and the Bay of Samaná. The protected waters of El Banco de la Plata y la Navidad are a sanctuary for the protection of mammalian animals in the Atlantic Ocean, including the humpback whales. During the winter, these giants of the sea migrate from the cold North into the warm Caribbean waters to mate and breed. This makes the Bay of Samaná one of the best places in the world to observe them! Whale-watching excursions are available January 15 - March 15.

ATV and Buggy Excursions

The guided ATV or buggy excursion is what the locals consider a “baño de pueblo” or “shower in the local culture tour”. This all-day experience is one of the most efficient and fun ways to explore our region, including: Boca del Diabo blowhole, the marble quarry, La Hondonada natural bridge, Playa Rincon, and the Rincon hinterland. Most of our guests choose the private tour option. Choosing between an ATV or a buggy depends on price and personal preference. On an ATV, two people sit one behind the other. On a buggy, you sit next to each other and are better protected with a roll cage, proper seats and seat belts.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is one of the foremost serene ways to encounter the shorelines, forests and wildernesses of Las Galeras. Whether you've never been on a horse or are an experienced rider, you’ll have an extraordinary encounter. We offer a half-day trip to the secluded Madama Beach, located within the Cabo Samaná National Park. For experienced riders, custom trips are available by inquiry, including the long but exceptionally fulfilling 7-beaches tour, which takes you all the way to Playa Rincon.

ZIP Line

Located 1 hour from Ocama, the Samaná Zipline is the perfect activity for individuals looking to get their adrenaline pumping. A hike through the mountains of El Valle culminates at a height of just over 350 feet high, kicking off your adventure through a spectacular journey over the tropical canopy. The amazing 12 cables and 13 platforms will leave you breathless and provides an extremely memorable experience, guaranteed! Finish off your adventure with a visit to one of the most majestic beaches of Samaná, Playa El Valle; a true paradise of sand and turquoise waters, surrounded by a lush tropical valley. If you do not have your own transportation, we typically organize a round-trip taxi for you.

Via Ferrata at Fronton

Live one of the most exhilarating experiences on the island, combining beach and mountain in one day: Fronton Ferrata. A protected climbing route equipped with fixed cables, ladders, and bridges,that allows climbers to safely combine the thrill of climbing with panoramic vistas of the coastline and surrounding landscape. Embark on the beautiful seafront from Las Galeras to Frontón and choose your adventure: climb the wall or walk to the lighthouse. End your adventure with a beach lunch contemplating the majestic Atlantic horizon.

Salto de Limon

Nature lovers can venture to El Limon waterfall, a natural reserve trail that takes you on a 4-kilometer hike down lush hilly terrain with a wealth of native flora and fauna. Once at the falls, marvel at the beauty of El Limon’s tumbling cascade, dropping 40 meters from the top of Sierra de Samaná. Cool off from the journey in the freshwater emerald pool beneath the falls, and take in the surrounding wild forest landscape and gushing sounds of El Limon. Our options run from self-guided walks to immersing in the local knowledge and culture with guided tours run by local professionals.

Birdwatching

A haven for birdwatchers, birdwatching in Samaná Bay is a delightful experience for nature enthusiasts and bird lovers alike, as the area's coastal mangroves and wetlands are home to a rich diversity of bird species. Birdwatching in Samaná Bay is excellent throughout the year, but the best time depends on your interests: endemic, resident, or migrant species. Ocama's lush surroundings offer the perfect place for some bird observation and identification. We also provide birdwatching tours in two of Samaná’s hotspots: El Limon Waterfall and Los Haitises National Park.

City Tours

Immersing in local life and sharing memories with its inhabitants is one of the greatest pleasures of traveling! Discover the rich history of Samaná and its people, as you enjoy a guided walk along the scenic Avenida la Marina and Pueblo Principe, and the iconic Iron Bridge. Alternatively, you can visit the local town of Las Terrenas, renowned for its vibrant atmosphere: picturesque beaches coupled with a mix of local Dominican culture and European influences. Here, you can explore colorful shops, enjoy delicious seafood at beachfront restaurants, or immerse yourself in the lively shops.

Ocama offers a unique retreat experience where guests can disconnect from the modern world and reconnect with nature in a luxurious and caring environment.
