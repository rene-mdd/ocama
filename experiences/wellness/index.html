WELLNESS

Pamper yourself, embrace tranquility, and indulge in a sensory journey that revitalizes your mind, body, and soul, with our luxurious spa treatments.

The surrounding forest at Ocama, allows us to transform your villa into a sanctuary of relaxation and healing. Experience pure renewal and rejuvenation as our expert therapists bring the essence of the spa to you, ensuring every moment is dedicated to your ultimate well-being. Whenever the weather permits, spa sessions are organized outside on your villa’s deck or at the beach, letting nature contribute to an even more intense experience.

MASSAGES

Massages are a powerful way to support your body's healing and immune system. Our spa team understands that every body is unique, which is why each massage and therapy session is catered to meet our guests’ specific requirements and to improve both physical and mental well-being

Deep Tissue Massage

A head-to-toe ritual that combines lymphatic drainage maneuvers with relaxation techniques for a body-relieving experience.

Relaxing Massage

Soothes the skin and the spirit. A subtle walk through your body evoking the movements of the waves and emphasizing specific relaxation points.

Aromatherapy Massage

Aromatherapy massage combines essential oils and soothing scents with a relaxing massage to ease tension and promote well-being. Discover a deeper sense of peace.

Kobido Massage

Kobido massage revitalizes your face, neck and shoulders with invigorating strokes. Reduce wrinkles, boost circulation and experience deep relaxation.

Lymphatic Drainage

Lighten your step, brighten your day! Lymphatic drainage massage uses gentle strokes to encourage fluid flow, reducing puffiness and promoting a sense of rejuvenation.

MASSAGE ENHANCEMENT

Allow us to extend your massage experience by incorporating these treatments for additional relaxation.

Scrubs

Indulge in the rejuvenating experience of our body scrub spa services, designed to refresh and revitalize your skin. Our meticulously crafted treatments exfoliate and hydrate, leaving your skin smooth, radiant, and deeply nourished.

From lavender, coconut, mud, cocoa, coffee or oats, we utilize premium, natural ingredients to ensure your skin receives the highest quality care. After your treatment, revel in the sensation of silky-smooth skin and a renewed sense of vitality.

Wraps

Our body wrap services are crafted to nourish your skin, improve circulation, and promote detoxification.

During your session, you'll be enveloped in a cocoon of carefully selected ingredients, tailored to meet your unique skin care needs and wellness goals. Feel the refreshing and cleansing effects of our body wraps, leaving you with a radiant glow and a profound feeling of well-being.

Reflexology

Based on the principle that reflex points on the feet are mirrors of the body's health status, reflexology focuses on the interconnectedness of the body's organs and systems through specific points on the feet. Through skilled manipulation of these points, reflexology aims to optimize physical and mental health by addressing imbalances and promoting a sense of rejuvenation.

Whether you're seeking relief from stress, pain management, or simply wish to enhance your health, reflexology offers a soothing and therapeutic escape.

SPECIAL PACKAGES

Romantic Couple Massage

Imagine a setting with flowers, petals, aromatic candles, sparkling wine, surrounded by nature’s sounds, and 2 therapists offering a relaxed massage.

Lavender Or Chocolate Therapy On The Beach

This package contains exfoliation, chocolate or lavender wrap, ocean bathing, and a relaxing massage with lavender cream or coconut oil.

BEAUTY SERVICES

Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to some well-deserved self-care, our beauty treatments ensure you feel pampered, rejuvenated, and glowing from head to toe.

Manicure & Pedicure

Enjoy meticulous nail care, including shaping, buffing, and cuticle care, followed by a relaxing massage to rejuvenate tired hands and feet. Choose from a variety of high-quality polishes or opt for a natural shine. Our treatments ensure your hands and feet not only look stunning but feel revitalized and nourished.

Facials

Whether you're targeting hydration, anti-aging, or deep cleansing, our facials are tailored to address your unique skincare concerns. Treat yourself to the ultimate relaxation with a facial massage and soothing mask as our estheticians cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish your skin to reveal a radiant complexion.

YOGA

Transform your villa space into a tranquil yoga sanctuary and experience the convenience of practicing yoga in the comfort of your villa away from distractions. Allow yourself to focus entirely on your practice, fostering a deeper connection with yourself and enhancing your overall well-being.

Self-Guided Practice

We provide yoga mats in your villa and a peaceful ambiance that will allow you to engage in movements that promote strength, flexibility, and relaxation.

Personalized Yoga Sessions

We also offer personalized yoga sessions, where our experienced yoga instructors will guide you through personalized sessions that cater to your individual needs and goals.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we tailor each session to accommodate your level of flexibility and fitness.

FITNESS

Stay motivated and energized with our in-villa fitness equipment. Dumbbells, weights, elastic bands, and yoga mats are available upon request and are delivered straight to your villa. Enjoy the flexibility of working out at your own pace and schedule, in the comfort of your villa.

We are located in a remote area, so spa and yoga sessions need to be arranged with sufficient prior notice. For spa sessions, we ask for at least 24 hours' notice. For yoga sessions, we ask for at least 48 hours, as the instructor is not always available.

Ocama offers a unique retreat experience where guests can disconnect from the modern world and reconnect with nature in a luxurious and caring environment.
