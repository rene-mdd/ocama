5-STAR SERVICES

At Ocama - The Villas at Rincon Bay, we want to invite our guests to experience the pinnacle of luxury by delivering 5-star personalized services that are designed to exceed your every expectation.

Discover a world where sophistication meets refined jungle comfort. Every detail is meticulously curated to provide you with an unforgettable experience of luxury and personalized indulgence.

Concierge Services

We offer tailored experiences that cater to your unique preferences and desires. Whether you seek arrangements for transportation, dining, spa services or personalized tours and/or need information about the local area, let us ensure every detail is meticulously planned.

Luxurious Accommodations

Indulge in elegantly built villas, each meticulously designed with exquisite furnishings and amenities to provide you with unparalleled comfort and relaxation. Enjoy breathtaking views, plush bedding, and facilities built to anticipate your needs.

Private Workspaces

In each villa you will find a dedicated workspace, built to provide a quiet and conducive environment for meetings, focused work or collaborative discussions. To ensure comfort during your work sessions, we offer a thoughtful layout: a work desk, high-speed internet access and a chair.

Business Center Services

We understand that many times travel requires taking care of business, At Ocama, you can enjoy high-speed internet connection (both wired and wireless), printing, scanning, and copying services.

Events

Looking to celebrate a special moment or a retreat? Our team can assist you with seamlessly planning for this occasion by organizing discounted room blocks,organizing services related to catering, decor, transportation and/or recreational activities for you and your guests.

Ocama offers a unique retreat experience where guests can disconnect from the modern world and reconnect with nature in a luxurious and caring environment.
