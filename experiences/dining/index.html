DINING AT OCAMA

Drawing from his Cocolo heritage, Chef Ray passionately explores the rich tapestry of his ancestors’ flavors, blending pre-Hispanic ingredients with contemporary flair.

Chef Ray Arthey

Born in the vibrant heart of Santo Domingo and refined in the culinary havens of Italy, Chef Ray Arthey brings a medley of flavors to Ocama. Inspired by his beloved grandmother Thelma, a culinary artisan, Ray mastered the art of balancing Dominican Creole cuisine with the elegance of Italian techniques.

Nourishing Cuisine

The result is a perfectly orchestrated symphony of unique flavors, vibrant colors, and intriguing textures. Experience a culinary journey where tradition meets innovation, crafted to tantalize your senses and elevate your dining experience to unparalleled heights.
