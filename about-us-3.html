    WHAT MAKES US SO SPECIAL?

    We invite you to escape the hectic pace of modern life and treat yourself to a truly distinctive travel experience.

    OCAMA, a 35 acre oceanfront luxury retreat, is perched on the verdant tropical hillsides above the stunning Bay of Rincon. Our intimate, boutique resort features just 8 private villas, each offering five star service, incomparable views and access to three exquisite beaches via enchanting jungle pathways. Seamlessly marrying luxury with the tropical outdoors, the property retains a feeling of peace and seclusion unmatched by larger luxury boutique resorts. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, enriched by exceptional amenities and personalized service. Leave your cares behind and embark on a rejuvenating journey unlike any other. Whether you're craving peace or adventure, we welcome you to disconnect, unwind, and allow yourself to be pampered in your own private sanctuary at Ocama.

    OUR VALUES

    Our core values serve as constant inspiration and guidance, reminding us of our identity and purpose. They represent our unwavering commitment to exceeding expectations, ensuring that we establish a sanctuary that authentically embodies our mission and values.

    Excellence

    We strive to deliver the highest standards in every aspect, from providing comfortable, elegant, and luxurious settings to offering exceptional dining experiences and guest services. Our aim is to ensure that our guests receive the very best of everything we have to offer.

    Caring Hospitality

    This is the heart of our service philosophy. We prioritize genuine care and warmth in every interaction, ensuring that each guest feels valued and welcomed. Our goal is to create memorable experiences through attentive service and thoughtful gestures that reflect our dedication to hospitality.

    Community Vibe

    At the core of our business, we prioritize people by actively engaging in our local community's development and fostering strong relationships with our guests and service providers. We believe in being an integral part of the community fabric, contributing positively, and nurturing meaningful connections with everyone we interact with.

    Earth Friendly

    Our retreat seamlessly blends into the local landscape and community, creating a peaceful oasis for people and wildlife and embracing environmentally-friendly practices, being socially responsible, and promoting sustainability.

    With just eight villas spread across 35 acres of lush forest, we have meticulously ensured minimal disruption to the delicate natural ecosystem. The eco-friendly design of our beautiful villas promotes a low environmental footprint, while providing casual, comfortable luxury.

    This is my happy place. It is the culmination of all my passions rolled into one exceptional travel destination; nature, cultural history, cuisine, architectural design and a laid back tropical vibe. Please enjoy all that the spectacular 35 acre Ocama retreat has to offer! My hope is that this becomes your happy place too!

    Much love,

    Mark Andrus

    Our Story

    Many years ago, our founder, Mark Andrus, embarked on an adventurous backpacking journey through Central America and the Caribbean in search of the most beautiful, tranquil, and healing place imaginable. After nine months of exploration, he finally discovered an oasis that was to eventually become Ocama.

    Creating this magnificent retreat took many years of planning and design to ensure that it would remain in harmony with the spectacular and delicate surrounding ecosystem. From hand-laid stone roads to homes carefully positioned along the natural contours of the hillsides, the aim was to collaborate with nature rather than disrupt it. Great care was taken to minimize environmental impact. Today, with our living green roofs and perimeters, the retreat coexists peacefully with its surroundings, seamlessly blending into the tropical forest.

    Each home is strategically positioned high on the hillsides to take advantage of the tropical breezes. Stairways, designed to enhance this natural cooling process in every room, run centrally through the residences. Simply slide open your doors and switch on your ceiling fan to live comfortably in sync with nature, minimizing the need for air conditioning.

    Our ongoing goal is to find innovative ways to preserve this remarkable natural habitat and contribute to the well-being of the earth.

    Ocama offers a unique retreat experience where guests can disconnect from the modern world and reconnect with nature in a luxurious and caring environment.
